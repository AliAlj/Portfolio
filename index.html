About

I am Ali Aljahmi, a Computer Science graduate from the University of Michigan Dearborn and a Junior Learner at the Apple Developer Academy. I enjoy building early stage digital products, learning from real feedback, and improving experiences through careful iteration.

One of my strengths is giving constructive feedback on early ideas by spotting small changes or alternative perspectives that meaningfully improve a project’s direction.

For the Digital Summer Clinic, I am excited by the idea of working closely with startups, shipping useful work quickly, and supporting decisions with data and clear communication.

What I bring

  • Thoughtful, constructive feedback on early ideas
  • Comfort working in ambiguous, iterative environments
  • Ability to turn complex work into clear outputs
  • Strong written and verbal communication
