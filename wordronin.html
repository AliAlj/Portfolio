WordRonin

A fast paced word game where I iterate on engagement through pacing, scoring, and real time player feedback.

iOS Gameplay loop Scoring design Accessibility

Overview

WordRonin is a word game where I treat every feature like an experiment. I design a mechanic, test how it feels, and then tighten the pacing and reward structure based on what players do, not what I assume they will do.

The work is centered on making the experience readable, responsive, and replayable. I am also developing an accessible listening focused mode that supports different ways of playing.

Current focus

Pacing, reward tuning, and accessible play modes that keep the loop satisfying without being noisy.

Outcome so far

  • Built core gameplay loop and iteration scaffolding
  • Implemented scoring experiments for rapid tuning
  • Designed toward accessible modes, including audio based play
  • Focused on clarity and feedback in every interaction
